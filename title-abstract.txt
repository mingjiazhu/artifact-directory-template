Title:
Model Analysis of Stock Price Trend Predictions based on Financial News

Abstract:
Financial news is an important source for people to learn information in financial field, such as the variation in stock market. News could also be a key factor to forecast change in the stock market. In this report, we introduce different methods we tried to predict the change of stock price by using financial news, including
Bag-of-Words, AutoPhrase, LSTM, and BERT. Our experiments demonstrate that BERT outperforms other models.
